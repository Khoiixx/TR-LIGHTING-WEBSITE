Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the lighting kits?

All our lighting kits come with detailed installation guides. The free version includes basic setup instructions, while premium versions include video tutorials and priority support.

Can I upgrade from v0.5 to v1 later?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. Contact us on Discord and we'll help you with the upgrade process and any questions about the transition.

Do you offer custom lighting designs?

Absolutely! We can create custom lighting configurations for your specific venue or event. Reach out on Discord to discuss your requirements.

What platforms are supported?

Our lighting kits work with most major event platforms and lighting systems. The MA2 version is specifically designed for professional MA2 controllers.
